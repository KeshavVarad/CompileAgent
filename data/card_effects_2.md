Here is the complete breakdown of all the cards featured in the video for the game Compile (covering Main 2 and Aux 2 sets), organized by protocol.
As explained in the video, a card's effects are split into three positions:
Top Command: Persistent effect
Middle Command: Immediate effect
Bottom Command: Auxiliary / End step effect
Chaos
Chaos 0
Top: None
Middle: Flip one covered card in each line.
Bottom (Start Step): Draw the top card of your opponent's deck; your opponent draws the top card of your deck.
Chaos 1
Top: None
Middle: Rearrange your protocols. Rearrange your opponent's protocols (you must change their current positions).
Bottom: None
Chaos 2
Top: None
Middle: Shift one of your covered cards.
Bottom: None
Chaos 3
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: This card may be played without matching protocols.
Chaos 4
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom (End Step): Discard your hand, then draw that many cards.
Chaos 5
Top: None
Middle: Discard one card.
Bottom: None
Clarity
Clarity 0
Top: Your total value in this line is increased by 1 for each card in your hand.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Clarity 1
Top (Start Step): Reveal the top card of your deck. You may discard it.
Middle: Your opponent reveals their hand.
Bottom: When this card would be covered, first draw 3 cards.
Clarity 2
Top: None
Middle: Reveal your deck. Draw 1 card with a value of 1 revealed this way, shuffle your deck, then play 1 card with a value of 1.
Bottom: None
Clarity 3
Top: None
Middle: Reveal your deck. Draw 1 card with a value of 5 revealed this way, then shuffle your deck.
Bottom: None
Clarity 4
Top: None
Middle: You may shuffle your trash into your deck.
Bottom: None
Clarity 5
Standard 5: Has the typical protocol 5 format (no top/bottom, middle is discard 1 card).
Corruption
Corruption 0
Top (Start Step): Flip 1 face-up covered or uncovered card in this stack other than this card.
Middle: None
Bottom: You may play this card in any line on either player's side (ignores protocols).
Corruption 1
Top: None
Middle: Return 1 card (uncovered only).
Bottom: When a card would be returned to your opponent's hand, put that card on top of their deck face down instead.
Corruption 2
Top: After you discard cards, your opponent discards 1 card.
Middle: Draw 1 card, discard 1 card.
Bottom: None
Corruption 3
Top: None
Middle: You may flip 1 face-up covered card.
Bottom: None
Corruption 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Corruption 6
Top (End Step): Either discard 1 card or delete this card.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Courage
Courage 0
Top (Start Step): If you have no cards in hand, draw 1 card.
Middle: Draw 1 card.
Bottom (End Step): You may discard 1 card. If you do, your opponent discards 1 card.
Courage 1
Top: None
Middle: Delete 1 of your opponent's cards in a line where they have a higher total value than you do.
Bottom: None
Courage 2
Top: None
Middle: Draw 1 card.
Bottom (End Step): If your opponent has a higher total value than you do in this line, draw 1 card.
Courage 3
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom (End Step): You may shift this card to the line where your opponent has their highest total value.
Courage 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Courage 6
Top (End Step): If your opponent has a higher value in this line than you do, flip this card.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Fear
Fear 0
Top: During your turn, your opponent's cards do not have middle commands.
Middle: Shift or flip 1 uncovered card.
Bottom: None
Fear 1
Top: None
Middle: Draw 2 cards. Your opponent discards their hand and draws the amount of cards discarded minus 1.
Bottom: None
Fear 2
Top: None
Middle: Return 1 of your opponent's uncovered cards.
Bottom: None
Fear 3
Top: None
Middle: Shift 1 of your opponent's covered or uncovered cards in this line.
Bottom: None
Fear 4
Top: None
Middle: Your opponent discards 1 random card.
Bottom: None
Fear 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Ice
Ice 1
Top: None
Middle: You may shift this card.
Bottom: After your opponent plays a card in this line, your opponent discards 1 card.
Ice 2
Top: None
Middle: Shift 1 other uncovered card (yours or your opponent's).
Bottom: None
Ice 3
Top (End Step): If this card is covered, you may shift it.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Ice 4
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: This card cannot be flipped.
Ice 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Ice 6
Top: If you have any cards in your hand, you cannot draw cards (Batched refreshes are still allowed if your hand is completely empty).
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Luck
Luck 0
Top: None
Middle: State a number. Draw 3 cards, reveal 1 card drawn with the face-up value of your stated number; you may play it.
Bottom: None
Luck 1
Top: None
Middle: Play the top card of your deck face down. Flip that card ignoring its middle effect.
Bottom: None
Luck 2
Top: None
Middle: Discard the top card of your deck. Draw cards equal to the value of the discarded card.
Bottom: None
Luck 3
Top: None
Middle: State a protocol. Discard the top card of your opponent's deck. If the discarded card matched the stated protocol, delete 1 uncovered card.
Bottom: None
Luck 4
Top: None
Middle: Discard the top card of your deck. Delete one covered or uncovered card that shares a value with the discarded card.
Bottom: None
Luck 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Mirror
Mirror 0
Top: Your total value in this line is increased by 1 for each of your opponent's cards in this line.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Mirror 1
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom (End Step): You may resolve the middle command of one of your opponent's uncovered cards as if it were on this card.
Mirror 2
Top: None
Middle: Swap all of your cards in one of your stacks with another one of your stacks (stacks must contain at least 1 card).
Bottom: None
Mirror 3
Top: None
Middle: Flip one of your uncovered cards. Flip one of your opponent's uncovered cards in the same line.
Bottom: None
Mirror 4
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: After your opponent draws cards, draw 1 card.
Mirror 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Peace
Peace 1
Top: None
Middle: Both players discard their hands.
Bottom (End Step): If your hand is empty, draw 1 card.
Peace 2
Top: None
Middle: Draw 1 card, then play 1 card from your hand face down.
Bottom: None
Peace 3
Top: None
Middle: You may discard 1 card to flip 1 uncovered card that has a value greater than the number of cards currently in your hand.
Bottom: None
Peace 4
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: After you discard cards during your opponent's turn, draw 1 card.
Peace 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Peace 6
Top: None
Middle: If you have more than one card in your hand, flip this card.
Bottom: None
Smoke
Smoke 0
Top: None
Middle: Play the top card of your deck face down in each line that contains a face-down card (checks both sides of the field).
Bottom: None
Smoke 1
Top: None
Middle: Flip one of your uncovered cards; you may shift that card.
Bottom: None
Smoke 2
Top: Your total value in this line is increased by 1 for each face-down card in this line.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Smoke 3
Top: None
Middle: Play 1 card face down in a line with an existing face-down card.
Bottom: None
Smoke 4
Top: None
Middle: Shift one covered face-down card.
Bottom: None
Smoke 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Time
Time 0
Top: None
Middle: Play 1 card from your trash, then shuffle your trash into your deck.
Bottom: None
Time 1
Top: None
Middle: Flip 1 covered card, then discard your entire deck.
Bottom: None
Time 2
Top: After you shuffle your deck, draw 1 card. Then, you may shift this card.
Middle: If there are any cards in your trash, you may shuffle your trash into your deck.
Bottom: None
Time 3
Top: None
Middle: Reveal 1 card from your trash. Play it face down in another line.
Bottom: None
Time 4
Top: None
Middle: Draw 2 cards, discard 2 cards.
Bottom: None
Time 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
War
War 0
Top: After you refresh, you may flip this card.
Middle: None
Bottom: After your opponent draws cards, you may delete one uncovered card.
War 1
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: After your opponent refreshes, discard any number of cards and refresh.
War 2
Top: None
Middle: Flip one uncovered card.
Bottom: After your opponent compiles, your opponent discards their hand.
War 3
Top: None
Middle: Draw 1 card.
Bottom: After your opponent discards cards, you may play one card face down.
War 4
Top: None
Middle: Your opponent discards 1 card.
Bottom: None
War 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Assimilation (Aux 2)
Assimilation 0
Top: None
Middle: Put one of your opponent's covered or uncovered field cards directly into your hand (counts as a placement, not a draw).
Bottom: None
Assimilation 1
Top: None
Middle: Discard 1 card, then refresh.
Bottom: After a player refreshes, draw the top card of your opponent's deck and discard 1 card into their trash.
Assimilation 2
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom (End Step): Play the top card of your opponent's deck face down in this stack.
Assimilation 4
Top: None
Middle: Draw the top card of your opponent's deck; your opponent draws the top card of your deck.
Bottom: None
Assimilation 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Assimilation 6
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom: Play the top card of your deck face down on your opponent's side of the field.
Diversity (Aux 2)
Diversity 0
Top: None
Middle: If there are 6 different protocols on face-up cards in the field, flip the Diversity protocol card directly to its compiled side (does not trigger a standard line-clear or card-steal).
Bottom (End Step): You may play one non-diversity card in this line.
Diversity 1
Top: None
Middle: Shift 1 uncovered card, then draw cards equal to the number of different protocols on cards in this line.
Bottom: None
Diversity 3
Top: Your total value in this line is increased by 2 if there are any non-diversity face-up cards in this deck.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Diversity 4
Top: None
Middle: Flip 1 uncovered card with a value less than the number of different protocols on face-up cards in the field.
Bottom: None
Diversity 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
Diversity 6
Top: If there are not at least 4 different protocols on face-up cards in the field, delete this card.
Middle: None
Bottom: None
Unity (Aux 2)
Unity 0
Top: None
Middle: If there is another Unity card in the field, either flip 1 uncovered card or draw 1 card.
Bottom: When this card would be covered by a Unity card, first either flip 1 uncovered card or draw 1 card.
Unity 1
Top (Start Step): If this card is covered, you may shift this card.
Middle: If there are 5 or more Unity cards in the field, flip the Unity protocol to the compiled side and delete all cards in that line.
Bottom: Unity cards may be played face up in this line.
Unity 2
Top: None
Middle: Draw cards equal to the number of Unity cards in the field.
Bottom: None
Unity 3
Top: None
Middle: If there is another Unity card in the field, you may flip one uncovered face-up card.
Bottom: None
Unity 4
Top: None
Middle: None
Bottom (End Step): If your hand is empty, reveal your deck, draw all Unity cards from it, then shuffle your deck.
Unity 5
Standard 5: Typical protocol 5 format.
