Here is the complete card breakdown and effect reference for the protocols featured in this video, covering the Main 1 (Core box) and Aux 1 sets for Compile.
Just like before, effects are split across three possible card positions:
Top Command: Persistent / Passive / Upkeep effects
Middle Command: Immediate effects executed when played or flipped face up
Bottom Command: Auxiliary / End-of-turn / Triggered effects
Note: For all text, "flip" or "delete" functions automatically target uncovered cards unless explicitly stating "all" or "covered."
